Recommendations for Improvement

The inclusive VT website looks well put together with strong topics in the toolbar and mostly informative sections throughout the website. However I feel there is some room for improvement. The events category is kind of dry, instead of just being a bulleted list you could add some information on a few events to inform the reader about the specifics of the event. Similarly the announcements section offers a list of recent announcement, to help improve that page there could be links to the new articles or videos, so the user can gain access to these announcements directly from the page. I would also recommend this for the DiversityEdu page so users can look through the site on their own, and any other sections where an outside source is referenced, for example the social media section in the Strong Together Initiative. In addition to adding links I would recommend adding some more photos to the sections that are lacking in information, this would help with the design of the website and help with the viewers interest in the topic. Overall the website has plenty of information on Inclusive VT and the facets that are involved, but the addition of links and images may help grab the users attention and provide even more information on the different topics.
