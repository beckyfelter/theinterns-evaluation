Peer Review Summary

Welcome to the peer review for InclusiveVT! “The Interns” group will be evaluating this website objectively from many perspectives. The “Quality Evaluation” of this peer review covers the technical aspects of the website’s content, accuracy, completeness, clarity, style, completeness, organization, retrievability, visual effectiveness and design. The level of quality evaluation is assessed using the rubrics from Developing Quality Technical Information. The “Usability” section covers the way the content would be viewed from the perspective of a student. The “Usability” section will verify the usability from a student’s perspective. This peer review closes with a suggestion of improvements that will assist the InclusiveVT group's ability to craft a more refined version of their content.
