Introduction to the InclusiveVT website evaluation

InclusiveVT is Virginia Tech’s commitment to advancing inclusion and diversity efforts on campus, which builds upon the university’s motto of Ut Prosim.

The website has four main tabs: “Home,” “For Students,” “For Faculty and Staff,” and “For Alumni.” The pages included on the homepage include several pages, describing what InclusiveVT is, providing a message from the VP of Strategic Affairs, and detailing upcoming events, news, and announcements.

The other tabs break down InclusiveVT for each audience: students, faculty/staff, and alumni. Each of these pages covers how the audience can get involved with the Virginia Tech’s commitment to diversity and inclusivity on campus. The student pages reiterate the commitment and describe ways students can get involved with it, such as attending events, taking online courses, or supporting the cause. The faculty/staff pages also detail available workshops, classes, and programs. The alumni pages focus on events, mentorship programs, board positions, and local events.

Our team will evaluate the InclusiveVT website based on quality and usability, as well as recommend areas for improvement.
